CASE STUDY 01 / RETAIL SCALE

Aura Cosmetic Brand Identity & Funnel Setup

4.2X ROAS Generated

Client Challenge:

The client was spending over ₹1.5L monthly on ads but suffering from high cart-abandonment rates. Their website lacked high-end brand appeal and their manual support team could not respond within 6 hours.

Our Strategy:

1. Restructure brand visual guides to match international luxury design trends.
2. Implement instant CRM WhatsApp routing triggers. As soon as a cart abandon happens, trigger a discount message within 90 seconds.

The Execution:

We designed high-end product catalogs, staged studio photoshoots, rebuilt the Shopify theme with clean glassmorphic grids, and connected Razorpay payment notifications to an automated HubSpot pipeline.

Results:

Cart abandonment recovery rate increased by 28%. Monthly revenue scaled from ₹1.5L to ₹5.2L in 90 days with zero operational additions.

CASE STUDY 02 / B2B AUTOMATION

Zenith Logistics Sales Pipeline Automation

Zero Admin Overheads

Client Challenge:

Incoming lead emails sat in mailboxes for hours. Sales reps spent 3 hours daily writing manual follow-ups, copying data to sheets, and scheduling booking calls.

Our Strategy:

Deploy a central Make.com scenario capturing mail details, creating Zoho CRM deals, scheduling slot timings, and dispatching calendar alerts to both client and sales rep.

The Execution:

We integrated Google Forms & Lead Gen Ads directly to Zoho CRM. Added automated WhatsApp calendar invitations. Set up automatic Slack notification pings to alert representatives when leads select slots.

Results:

Lead contact speed improved from 4 hours to 8 seconds. Sales representatives closed 35% more deals due to immediate customer engagement.
